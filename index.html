About

Prosanta Barai (1964-2020) was the eldest son of Rasmohan Barai. He was educated from Paisa High School And College. In his mature years, in addition to his many-sided activities, he managed the family estates, a project which brought him into close touch with common humanity and increased his interest in social reforms.He had early success as a Rural Doctor in his native .with his as result of his efficient treatment he became rapidly known in his village area. In fact his fame attained a luminous height.

Rural doctor (Positive attitude)

He was born in 1971, in Somairpar village of Agailjhara upazila of Barisal district.His father's name was Rasmohan Barai and he was a doctor by profession. His mother's name was Sarojini Barai and she was a housewife by profession. And he got married on 12 August 1997 with Shikha Rani Biswas. And his wife is a school teacher by profession. Prosanta Barai has two sons, elder son named Prothes Barai and younger son named Angkan Barai.
